AI Think Tank Competition

Competition: UNSW AI Think Tank

Project Focus: Cybersecurity, AI Solutions

Role: Contributed as a Solutions Architect in collaboration with Sarah Lim and Tianyi Angela Lin

Outcome: 1st Place

I had the pleasure of presenting in the finalist round of the Artificial Intelligence Think Tank Case Competition, organized by UNSW Capital W, UNSW Digital Society, and UNSW Women in Technology. I am proud to share that, in partnership with Sarah Lim and Tianyi Angela Lin, our team achieved First Place for our project: Cyber Central.

Cyber Central is a centralised cybersecurity attack simulator and training platform that leverages generative AI to dispatch auto-generated and customizable phishing simulations. This solution enhances employee awareness and resilience against phishing and malware attacks. It was a fantastic opportunity to apply academic knowledge and collaborate with like-minded individuals who share my passion for Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity.

Project Highlights:

  • Won first place at the UNSW AI Think Tank Case Competition
  • Developed Cyber Central, a platform that provides realistic phishing simulations using AI
  • Contributed as a Solutions Architect in collaboration with Sarah Lim and Tianyi Angela Lin to present this innovative solution
